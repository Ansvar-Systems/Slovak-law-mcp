## Data Authority and Limitations

### Source Authority

This Tool aggregates data from official Slovak legal databases. However:

| Source Type | Authority Level | Use Case |
|------------|----------------|----------|
| **slov-lex.sk** | Authoritative | Statute text, official collection (primary reference) |
| **Zbierka zákonov SR (Collection of Laws)** | Authoritative | Original official publication |
| **Community/open-data sources** | Supplementary | Cross-references, metadata (verify independently) |
| **EUR-Lex / treaty databases** | Authoritative for EU/international law | EU directives, regulations, international obligations |

**CRITICAL**: Community-maintained resources are **NOT** official government publications. While generally reliable, they may contain transcription errors, outdated information, or incomplete coverage.

### Commercial Legal Databases

For professional legal work, **always cross-check with commercial legal databases** such as:

- ASPI (Automatizovaný systém právních informací), EPI.sk, Magister

These services provide editorial oversight, comprehensive annotations (komentáre), and professional-grade currency guarantees that this Tool **cannot** provide.

## Currency and Staleness

### No Real-Time Updates

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by **weeks or months**
- Slovak law changes continuously through new statutes (zákony), amendments (novely), and court decisions (rozhodnutia)
- **Last-updated timestamps** in tool responses indicate data age, but should be treated as unreliable

### Staleness Warnings

The Tool includes staleness warnings when data is >30 days old. **These warnings are not exhaustive**:

- Even "fresh" data may be outdated if recent amendments were published in the Zbierka zákonov SR
- Staleness warnings do not guarantee completeness or accuracy
- Always check official sources for the current legal position (aktuálny právny stav)

### Amendments and Repeals

- A provision marked "in force" may have been:
  - Amended (novelizovaný) after the last database update
  - Repealed (zrušený) but not yet removed from the database
  - Subject to transitional rules (prechodné ustanovenia) affecting its applicability
- The Tool does **NOT** automatically track amendments in real-time

## Coverage Gaps

### What's Missing

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Complete case law** -- Coverage of the Supreme Court (Najvyšší súd SR), Constitutional Court (Ústavný súd SR), and lower court decisions is limited
2. **Historical statute versions** -- Limited availability of historical provision wording (historické znenie predpisu)
3. **Legal commentary** -- No editorial annotations, academic commentary (komentáre, monografie), or practice guides
4. **Unpublished decisions** -- Administrative rulings largely absent
5. **Full legislative history** -- Limited coverage of parliamentary materials (dôvodové správy, správy výborov NR SR)

### Impact on Professional Use

These gaps mean:

- **Incomplete Legal Research**: Tool results are inherently incomplete and may miss critical authorities
- **Context Missing**: Without commentary and legislative materials, interpretation may be incorrect
- **EU Law Blind Spots**: Cannot fully assess EU law obligations or treaty compliance
